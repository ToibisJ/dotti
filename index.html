AI support for calmer parenting days.

Dotti turns scattered parenting pressure into a guided, supportive daily experience: reflection, planning, reassurance, and next steps in one friendly product system.

Product idea

From chaos to calm, one assisted step at a time.

Dotti is designed around the emotional load of parenting: many small decisions, limited energy, and a need for support that feels immediate without becoming clinical or cold.

The visual language balances a playful character with structured flows, giving the product warmth while keeping the interface clean enough for repeated daily use.

Research signals

What the case study surfaces

01

Parents need relief from mental clutter.

The experience emphasizes quick orientation, gentle prompts, and visible progress instead of dense dashboards.

02

Trust matters before automation.

The design uses human wording, transparent task cards, and small guided actions to make AI feel supportive.

03

Daily rhythm beats one-time setup.

Flows are organized around recurring needs: overview, reflection, selected tasks, and assisted recommendations.
